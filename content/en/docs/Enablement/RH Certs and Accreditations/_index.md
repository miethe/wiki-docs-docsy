## Obtain RHLS
If you are interested in getting a Red Hat Learning Subscription (RHLS), see the [W3 site here](https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/gbs-na-l-k-multicloud-enablement/red-hat-certification#obtaininganrhls).
